Course Description: Discrete Maths(MAT102)

Course Credit :3 
Faculty : Srikrishnan Divakaran


This course aims to provide a rigorous introduction to the elementary concepts in Discrete Mathematics. 
Topics include: Foundations of Logic and Proofs, Sets and Sequences,
Algorithms and its analysis, Induction and Recursion, Counting, Relations, Trees, and Graphs.

Course Objectives:
A rigorous introduction to the elementary concepts in Discrete Mathematics.

Apply elementary concepts in Discrete Mathematics to solve problems with applications in Computer Science
