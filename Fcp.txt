Course Description: Foundamental Of Computer Programming (FCP)

Course Credit :3 
Faculty : KuntalKumar Patel

The course is aimed to give the exposure to programming paradigms and to develop the problem solving ability.
The course would introduce the concepts of computer programming. 
Following topics would be covered during the course:
Problem solving using Algorithms and Flowcharts; Programming paradigms; Foundations of Python and C programming;
Visual programming; Operators; Control statements; Input/output operations; Decision making and branching; 
Type conversions; Collections; Functions; String; File management; Exception handling.

Course Objectives
- To introduce fundamentals of computer programming.
- To develop problem solving and critical thinking ability.
- To understand the process of designing and implementing computer programs to 
solve various engineering and computing problems.
